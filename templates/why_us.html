Kidney Diseases

Overview

There are various types of diseases such as Chronic Kidney Diseases, Kidney Stones, Glomerulonephritis, Polycystic kidney diseases, Urinary tract infections and many more. Large number of population especially in India, are suffering through these vulnerable diseases especially one's who had to undergo dialysis treatment regularly.

Features this project endorses with:

  • Kidney Disease Predictor is provided for individuals to check if they are prone to kidney diseases.
  • Kidney Stone Prediction to check if the patient has stones formed in their kidney once again using ML.
  • Visualizations are provided to showcase how it's differentiated when considering a patient who is normal, suffering from kidney disease, inclulcated with kidney stones, who is undergoing dialysis treatment.
  • We have a separate section of Tips to prevent from kidney related diseases or to overcome it for better.

Solution

This project would be an asset to all the individuals who are prone to or patients who are undergoing various medical ailments for kidney related diseases.This would be a one stop place to check on with their health conditions and as well get good guidance on same to overcome this predominant issue.
